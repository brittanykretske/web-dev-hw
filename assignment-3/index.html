Easy Biscuits and Gravy

"Hot jumbo buttermilk biscuits with creamy sausage gravy are ready in just 15 minutes for a hearty, family-favorite breakfast."

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 oz) can refrigerated jumbo buttermilk biscuits
  • 1 (9.6 oz) package of Jimmy DeanÂ® Original Hearty Pork Sausage Crumbles
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 21/2 cups milk
  • Salt and Pepper (to taste)

Directions

  1. Prepare biscuits according to package description.
  2. While the biscuits are baking, cook sausage in large skillet over medium heat for 5-6
    minutes or until thoroughly heated and browned, stirring frequently. Stir in flour. Gradually add milk.
  3. Cook until mixture comes to a boil and thickens, stirring constantly. Reduce heat to medium-low;
    simmer 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Split biscuits in half. Place 2 halves on each of 8 plates; top with about 1/3 cup gravy.
