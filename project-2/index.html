How To Sew a Ladder Stitch

How often do you find that you have tears along the seams of your t-shirts or holes forming in your favorite sweater? Don'sweat sweat it, just sew it! The Ladder stitch, also known as the Invisible titch, easily fixes seams without showing the thread. You'll never even know there used to be a tear!
