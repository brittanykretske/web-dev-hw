About Me

My name is Brittany Kretske and I am currently a Bachelor of Arts student at the University of Montana. I love to do everything and anything creative from art to crafts, photography, and even theatre. I have a passion to create and to push my imaginition and I love nothing more than be able to enjoy and be proud of the things that I have made myself.

I have chosen to do the Ladder Stitch as my instructable for Project 2 because I find this sewing technique to be a life-saver! It can be used for all sorts of purposes including but not limited to creating blind stitches by hand, fixing holes, tears, or unravelled seams, and taking in fabric to a smaller width. This instructable is also a good representation of some of the hobbies that I enjoy doing in my spare time.
